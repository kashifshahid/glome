FAQs

Is Glome free?
Yes — the core Glome app is completely free. You can claim your home, receive neighbour alerts, use Walk With Me, and file council reports at no cost. Premium is an optional subscription for dedicated patrol support.
Do I need a card to join?
You'll need a confirmation card to fully verify your address. After signing up, we mail a card with a unique code. Enter it in the app and your home lights up on the map.
Is my exact address public?
No. Your exact address is never shared publicly. Neighbours can see that a verified home exists nearby, but your precise house number remains private. You control what information is visible and to whom.
How local is it?
Very. Glome is built around your immediate neighbourhood — typically a few streets in each direction. You'll only see and hear from people who live close by.
What can neighbours reply with?
Neighbours can acknowledge alerts, add context, or mark them as resolved. Responses are visible to the local network so everyone stays informed.
Can I upgrade to Premium later?
Absolutely. Start with the free plan and upgrade at any time from within the app. No commitment required to get started.
